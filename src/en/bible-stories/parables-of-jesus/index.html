The Parables of Jesus Christ

In this section we have collected the main parables of our Lord Jesus Christ. They are divided into 5 thematic groups. By reading them regularly, you can better understand our Savior and become wiser.

1. The parables of the Christian qualities

To become a Christian does not mean to agree with Christian knowledge . Through his parables, Jesus speaks of the profound inner transformation of personality that must take place in all who want to become his disciple.

The Sower

Our symbolic heart influences the acceptance of truth

4. The Parables for Christians living in the last days

After his death, Jesus rose from the dead and ascended to heaven. And on earth, he left his Church, which should preach the Good News to all mankind. Jesus also warned that the time of his second coming would be a time of judgment for both the ungodly people of this world and the hypocritical Christians. These parables explain why, for example, among the true servants of God there are false ones. It is also said how the division into the pure and the impure will be conducted at the end.
