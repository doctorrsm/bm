The Parable of the Pearl

(Mt 13:45-46)

45 “Again, the Kingdom of Heaven is like a man who is a merchant seeking fine pearls, 46 who having found one pearl of great price, he went and sold all that he had and bought it.

(Matthew 13:45,46)
